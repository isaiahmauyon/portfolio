(Design Process)

How I Design

01. Research & Discovery

Every project begins with understanding the problem. I research user needs, study competition, and define goals to set a strong foundation.

02. Ideation & Wireframing

I translate ideas into low-fidelity sketches and wireframes, focusing on structure, flow, and user journeys before jumping into visuals.

03. Visual Design

Once the foundation is clear, I bring concepts to life with clean, modern, and user-friendly interfaces that align with brand identity.

04. Development Handoff

I prepare pixel-perfect designs, style guides, and assets, working closely with developers for a smooth design-to-code transition.
